Top 10 Beaches in West Zone


Grace Bay Beach

"Grace Bay Beach, located on Providenciales in the Turks and Caicos Islands, is a world-renowned beach known for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear turquoise water, and calm, shallow waters."


Seven Mile Beach,

"Seven Mile Beach, located on the western side of Grand Cayman, is a world-renowned stretch of white sand and turquoise water known for its calm, clear waters and gentle waves. It's a popular destination for swimming, sunbathing, and a variety of water sports. "


Balandra Beach,

"Balandra Beach, located in La Paz, Baja California Sur, Mexico, is renowned for its pristine white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and shallow, calm depths, making it a popular destination for visitors and a photographer's dream. The beach is known for its natural beauty and is part of a protected area, ensuring its unspoiled state. "


Eagle Beach,

"Eagle Beach in Aruba is renowned as a world-class beach, consistently ranked among the best in the Caribbean and globally. It features a long stretch of pristine white sand, soft and powdery, and clear, warm turquoise waters. The beach is known for its Fofoti trees (a unique tree species) and is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike. "


Pink Sands Beach,

"Pink Sands Beach, located on Harbour Island in the Bahamas, is famous for its unique, pink-hued sand. This distinct color comes from microscopic coral insects called Foraminifera, which have bright pink or red shells. The sand is a mixture of coral, shells, and calcium carbonate, with the foraminifera shells contributing to the pink color. The beach is generally described as being a pale pink color, verging on a coral shade. "


Copacabana Beach,

Copacabana Beach, a 4-kilometer stretch of coastline in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, is renowned as one of the world's most famous beaches. It's characterized by its wide, sandy beach, calm waters, and the iconic black and white wave pattern promenade designed by Roberto Burle Marx. The beach is a hub for both locals and tourists, offering activities like swimming, sunbathing, playing beach sports, and enjoying refreshments from kiosks along the promenade. ""


Playa Norte,

Playa Norte, on Isla Mujeres near Cancun, is a renowned beach known for its pristine white sand, crystal-clear turquoise water, and lush palm trees. It's a popular destination for travelers seeking a relaxed beach experience, offering opportunities for swimming, snorkeling, and enjoying the Caribbean Sea. ""


Matira Beach,

"Matira Beach, Bora Bora, French Polynesia, is a popular, public beach known for its pristine white sand, turquoise waters, and lush palm trees. It's the only public beach on the island and is located on the southern tip, also known as Matira Point. The beach is ideal for sunbathing, swimming, and snorkeling, with rays and even black tip sharks occasionally visible in the lagoon"


Cannon Beach,

"Cannon Beach is a coastal city in Oregon, known for its stunning scenery, particularly the iconic Haystack Rock. It's a popular tourist destination, recognized for its beauty and art scene. The beach itself is a sandy stretch, extending nearly four miles and offering various activities like beachcombing and birdwatching. "


La Concha Beach

"La Concha Beach, located in San Sebastian, Spain, is a renowned urban beach celebrated for its crescent-shaped bay, fine golden sand, and clear waters. It's a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports, offering a variety of amenities and services. "
