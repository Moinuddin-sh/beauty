Top 10 Beaches in East Zone


Cox's Bazar Beach,

"Cox's Bazar Beach, located in Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh, is renowned as the longest natural sea beach in the world, stretching over 120 kilometers along the Bay of Bengal. It's a popular destination for travelers seeking a unique beach experience"


Anse Source d'Argent

"Anse Source d'Argent is a famous beach located on La Digue Island in the Seychelles, known for its stunning natural beauty and often called the most photographed beach in the world. It is characterized by white sand, turquoise waters, and large granite boulders, creating a unique and picturesque landscape. Access to the beach is through the L'Union Estate, a historic coconut plantation, adding to its appeal and cultural significance"


Flamenco Beach in Culebra, Puerto Rico

"Flamenco Beach in Culebra, Puerto Rico, is renowned for its pristine white sand, calm turquoise waters, and is consistently ranked among the world's best beaches. The beach's sheltered, horseshoe-shaped bay creates ideal conditions for swimming, snorkeling, and simply relaxing on the shore. It's also index to iconic rusty World War II tanks, a unique and photo-worthy feature. "


Radhanagar Beach

"Radhanagar Beach on Havelock Island (also known as Swaraj Dweep) in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands is renowned for its pristine white sand, clear turquoise waters, and lush green surroundings. It's a popular destination for sunbathing, swimming, and exploring the vibrant marine life through snorkeling and scuba diving. The beach is also known for its spectacular sunsets and is surrounded by tropical forests, creating a tranquil and serene atmosphere"


White Beach in Boracay,

"White Beach in Boracay, Philippines is famous for its powdery white sand and calm, clear turquoise waters, making it a popular destination for swimming and sunbathing. It's a long stretch of beach, roughly 4 kilometers (2.5 miles), and is lined with resorts, restaurants, and other tourism-related businesses. The beach is divided into three "stations" (Station 1, Station 2, and Station 3), each with its own distinct atmosphere and activities."


Gili Air is a small,

"Gili Air is a small, idyllic Indonesian island in the Gili Islands group, known for its white sand beaches, turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life. It's a popular destination for snorkeling and diving, particularly for those seeking to swim with sea turtles. Gili Air is also known for its laid-back atmosphere and is considered a peaceful escape, offering a balance between relaxation and nightlife. "


Nacpan Beach,

"Nacpan Beach, located in El Nido, Philippines, is a 3-kilometer-long golden sand beach known for its stunning natural beauty and tranquil atmosphere. It's a popular destination for day trips from El Nido, offering a variety of activities like swimming, sunbathing, and walking along the shore"


Kelingking Beach,

"Kelingking Beach, also known as "T-Rex Beach," is a must-see attraction on Nusa Penida Island, Bali, Indonesia. It's famous for its distinctive viewpoint at the top of the cliffs, offering a bird's-eye perspective that resembles a Tyrannosaurus Rex. The beach itself is a secluded, pristine area with turquoise waters and white sand, accessible by a steep hike down the cliffs


Hidden Beach,

"Hidden Beach in Hoi An, Vietnam is a quieter, less touristy alternative to the popular Cua Dai and An Bang beaches. Tucked away in the middle of these two, it's easily accessible via a narrow track off the main road. Known for its serene atmosphere, lush natural surroundings, and clear waters, it's a great spot for relaxation and enjoying the coastal beauty"
